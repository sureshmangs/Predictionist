What is JoSAA

The Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA) 2018 is responsible for counselling and seat allocation of candidates who will clear JEE Main 2018 and JEE Advanced 2018. JoSAA 2018 will conduct counselling for JEE Main 2018 qualifying candidates and will allot seats for admissions to the participating institutes, which consist of NITs, IIITs and other-GFTIs. Apply for NDA 2018

NDA (I) Application Form 2018 can be filled till 5th February 2018. National Defence Academy (NDA Exam) is the national level examination. This exam is conducted two times within a period of one year. NDA Exam is managed by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC). This examination is conducted for the recruitment in Indian Defence Forces which include Indian Army, Air Force and Navy.
